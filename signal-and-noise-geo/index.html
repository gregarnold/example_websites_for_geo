What Signal & Noise GEO does for brands

We specialize in Generative Engine Optimization: the practice of structuring content so AI models can find it, understand it, and recommend it to users.

GEO Audits

We analyze every page of your site against the structural factors that determine AI visibility: content quality, schema markup, passage self-containment, fact density, and citation readiness. You get a score, a prioritized fix list, and a clear path to improvement. We use GeoScored as our primary audit instrument.

Content Optimization

We rewrite and restructure your existing content to maximize AI citation probability. This means self-contained passages, answer-first structure, high fact density, and proper heading hierarchy. Every edit is measurable.

Structured Data Strategy

Schema markup, llms.txt, robots.txt, and sitemap configuration that tells AI crawlers exactly what your content is about. We implement Organization, Article, FAQPage, HowTo, and Product schemas tailored to your content types.

AI Visibility Monitoring

Ongoing tracking of how AI models reference your brand. We monitor citation frequency, recommendation context, and competitive positioning across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude to catch changes before they impact your pipeline.
