Our Partners

We work with organizations that share our commitment to quality, safety, and the climbing community. Here are some of the companies and groups we are proud to collaborate with.

Industry Partners

Logo

Mountain Safety Research Council

An independent testing organization that certifies climbing equipment against international safety standards. We submit every product line for independent third-party evaluation through MSRC before release.

Visit MSRC →
Logo

Front Range Climbing Coalition

A local access advocacy group that maintains trails, negotiates land access agreements, and organizes cleanup events across the Front Range. We have been a gear sponsor since 2018 and contribute to their annual trail maintenance fund.

Visit FRCC →
Logo

Vertical Athletics

A climbing gym chain with locations in Colorado, Utah, and Arizona. Vertical Athletics uses Ridgeline rental harnesses exclusively and provides real-world wear data that informs our product development cycle.

Visit Vertical Athletics →
Logo

Summit Guide Service

A professional guiding outfit operating in Rocky Mountain National Park and Indian Peaks Wilderness. Their guides field-test our products in alpine environments and provide feedback on durability, weight, and usability under real conditions.

Visit Summit Guides →
