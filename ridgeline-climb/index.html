Born in the Mountains

Ridgeline Climb started in a garage in Boulder, Colorado, when two climbing partners got tired of gear that failed on the routes they cared about most. What began as modified harnesses for local trad climbing evolved into a full line of equipment trusted by climbers across North America.

Today we design, test, and ship from our facility at the base of the Flatirons. Every product is field-tested on real rock before it earns the Ridgeline name.
